Barcelona close in on LaLiga title after win over Osasuna

Barcelona overcame LaLiga's controversial decision to schedule a late match on gameweek 27 just after the end of the World Cup qualifiers and Nations League quarter-finals.

Hansi Flick's side beat Osasuna 3-0 with goals from Ferrán Torres, Dani Olmo and Robert Lewandowski at the Montjuic Olympic Stadium. Even without Raphinha (rest due to physical exhaustion), Barcelona needed just 11 minutes to open the scoring, with Baldé finding Ferrán Torres on a cross after a beautiful exchange of passes. Where and when to watch the 2024 derby between Real Betis and Sevilla FC?

The Benito Villamarín Stadium hosts an exciting derby between Real Betis and Sevilla FC.

The BIG DERBY rivalry translates to a duel that could be decisive in the aspirations of both teams. In this game, the Green-and-Whites will hope to achieve a new triumph that will bring them closer to the qualification for European competitions. In the meanwhile, the Sevillistas will look to distance themselves almost definitively from the relegation positions. The return leg of THE BIG DERBY of the 2023/24 campaign is scheduled for Sunday, April 28. It will be disputed at the Benito Villamarín Stadium and will be broadcast worldwide in 190 countries.

Real Betis vs Sevilla FC will be played at 21:00, and this match corresponds to the 33rd round of LALIGA EA SPORTS. It will be the second derby between the two teams this season.

Umar Sadiq, LALIGA EA SPORTS Best Goal in March.

Umar Sadiq, the Nigerian player from Valencia CF, has been chosen for his back-heeled goal against CA Osasuna as the Best LALIGA EA SPORTS Goal of March.

The striker conjured up the most spectacular goal of the whole month with an incredible finish with his back to goal that helped his team to scrape a 3-3 draw in their visit to El Sadar. The match was going 3-2 to Osasuna and it was the 88th minute of the game when, after a cross into the area from Sergi Canós, Sadiq rose with his back to the goal and connected with a back-heeled shot that surprised the opposition goalkeeper and established the final 3-3 draw for his team.
